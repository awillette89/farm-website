Ordering Process

At Smokehouse Farms, we want to make your ordering experience simple. Here’s how it works:

  1. Contact Us: Message us on Facebook or send us an email.
  2. Custom Order:You may choose to preorder or order from what is in stock (typically whole birds in the 3+ lb range and eggs).
  3. Pickup Your Chicken:Come to the farm to collect your chicken. Depending on location, delivery may be available.

Why Choose Smokehouse Farms?
